About the author

I've been the patient.
And the practitioner.

I grew up in Bergen, Norway, obsessed with football from the moment I could kick a ball. My dad was my first coach. I was never the most talented player — I just worked harder than everyone else. Coaches told me I was too small. I kept going anyway, eventually becoming one of the youngest players to debut at senior level in Norway.

Then I broke my ankle. Tore all the ligaments. And never recovered properly. I fell into depression, gained weight, and stopped caring about my body entirely. It was my dad who pushed me toward a gym — and the personal trainer I found there who changed my life. After 100+ sessions together, I enrolled in the same education he had, qualified as a PT, and never looked back.

Over 13 years, I've helped hundreds of people understand their bodies, resolve chronic pain, and move better. Then two years ago, I was hit simultaneously by COVID-19 and mononucleosis — and was subsequently diagnosed with Myalgic Encephalomyelitis. For the first time, I experienced everything I'd been teaching from the inside. This guide is built from all of it.
